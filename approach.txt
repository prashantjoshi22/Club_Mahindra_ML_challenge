The complete competation was revolving around creating the features from the data set
Features created
	1) Day,month,year from the checkin date
	2)Number of weekend falling during the trip
	3)Seasons were extracted during the check in date
	4)Is the person was travelling in same state or not
	5)Giving rating to a particular resort
	6)yearly visit number of a particular member
	7)Date difference between booking date and checkin date
	8)Child to person ratio

All the catagorical features were converted into polynomial encoging
LightGBM is used to train the model
